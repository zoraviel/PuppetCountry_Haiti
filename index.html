Haiti: A Puppet Country

Scroll up or down

A look inside Haiti's history with foriegn powers through its presidents.

Haiti is known as being the first slave nation to gain its independence from its captors. However, its sovereignty is still questionable. It is considered the poorest country within the Western Hemisphere and continues to receive aid in the form of donations, military and political advisement. The country shows little sign of economic growth, despite the constant presence of foreign power. From the very start, Haiti has been dealing with "superpower" countries with little gain.
